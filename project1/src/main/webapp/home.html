About KL University

The Koneru Lakshmaiah Charities was established as a trust in 1980 with its official address at Museum road, Vijayawada. The KL College of Engineering began in 1980-81 and later became the Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation in 1996. The college attained autonomous status in 2006 and was recognized as a Deemed to be University in 2009, named KL University. Situated on a spacious 50-acre campus on the banks of the Buckingham Canal, near Vijayawada city, the campus is a haven of natural beauty and serenity, aptly named "Green Fields". The campus covers a built-up area of around 1,500,000 sq. ft. K.L. University offers graduate and post-graduate programs in engineering and is backed by excellent R&D. It is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) of AICTE, with six branches accredited for 5 years.
